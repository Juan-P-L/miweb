Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy (Magnetic Drawing Board. Namespace: project.jp.canvaskids)

Thank you for using our children's drawing application. At 'Magnetic Drawing Board', we are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect information in our application.

Data Security

At 'Magnetic Drawing Board', we do not collect or store personally identifiable information such as names, addresses, or phone numbers from our users. We also do not access or collect sensitive information.

Data Collection and Usage

Our application allows users to draw and use predefined drawings of animals that make sounds. We do not collect or store any drawings created by users as we do not offer a save function.

Advertisements

To keep our application free, we display advertisements through AdMob. We ensure that the ads are suitable for children and comply with appropriate content guidelines. These ads may be based on the application's content and do not collect personally identifiable information.

Manifest Permissions

Our application requires the following manifest permissions to function properly:

  • Internet permission: Required to enable ad loading and interaction with the AdMob server.
  • Advertising ID permission: Required to display personalized ads to users.
Data Security and Retention

We take reasonable measures to ensure the security of user data. We do not share personal information with third parties or transfer data to our servers.
